The Collective

A members-only space where golfers come to enjoy the game, keep fit, and feel at home.

We're not a corporate gym or a stuffy golf club. We're a small, friendly facility built around two things: great golf simulators and a decent gym. Simple as that. Whether you're sneaking in a quick session before work, grinding away at your swing on a rainy afternoon, or just getting some reps in, this is your space to use however works best for you.

We keep our membership deliberately small. That means you'll always get your slot, the gym won't be rammed, and over time you'll get to know the faces around you. It's that kind of place.

Getting in is as easy as it gets — no fuss, no front desk, just keyless access so you can show up whenever suits you, any time from 5am to midnight.
