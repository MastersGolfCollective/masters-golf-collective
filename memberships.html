A few things worth knowing

Capped membership. We deliberately limit the number of members to ensure you always get your slot and the gym never feels overcrowded.

Monthly rolling. No long tie-ins. Cancel anytime with 30 days notice. Annual memberships are paid upfront and save you the equivalent of 2 months.

Simulator bookings. Slots are booked in advance via the members portal. Unused monthly allowances do not roll over to the following month.

Keyless access. No front desk, no fuss. Once you're a member you'll receive your access credentials and can use the facility any time between 5am and midnight.
